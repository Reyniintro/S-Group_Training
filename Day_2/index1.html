Kimetsu no Yaiba

Demon Slayer (Kimetsu no Yaiba) is a famous Japanese manga/anime series created by Koyoharu Gotouge, revolving around the journey of young Tanjiro Kamado as he becomes a Demon Slayer to save his demon-turned sister Nezuko and avenge his family. The series is renowned for its emotional storyline and top-tier combat animation by studio ufotable. In Demon Slayer, the Demon Slayers fight using a highly distinctive power system, most notably Breathing Styles and advanced sword techniques.

  • Sun Breathing (Breathing Styles): This is the primary "power source" of the Demon Slayers. Each user adopts a different breathing style (e.g., Flame Breathing, Stone Breathing, Wind Breathing...). When utilized, they generate high-speed slashes/forms that carry the visual effects of that specific "breathing rhythm". Later on, some can achieve advanced variations (special forms, higher speed/control capabilities).
  • Instincts – anti-demon combat skills. Demon Slayers are typically trained to: Read the demon's movements (parry, dodge, and counterattack). Attack weak points accurately (e.g., strikes aimed at body parts to prevent demons from regenerating/transforming).
  • Advanced Sword Techniques (Essence of "Advanced Sword Skills"). Certain abilities/forms act as "exclusive combat skills": Combining the weapon + tempo + slashing techniques to maximize effectiveness. This heavily depends on personal skill and the level of training.
  • Hierarchy of "ranks" (from standard to Hashira). Within the organization, power is demonstrated through: Physical fitness and breath control. Technical proficiency. High-ranking characters (such as the Hashira) use extremely powerful and refined attacks.
  • Unique mechanic: "Surpassing limits". In certain cases, Demon Slayers can: Temporarily enhance their strength to push past their physical limits. However, this comes at the cost of potential physical injury (depending on the character's situation).
